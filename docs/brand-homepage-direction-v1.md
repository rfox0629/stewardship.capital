# Brand Homepage Direction v1

## Brand Position

Stewardship Capital is a Christian first Stewardship Operating System for families, business owners, investors, doctors, dentists, real estate owners, and affluent Christian households.

Core promise:

Understand, organize, and grow everything God has entrusted to you.

## Brand Direction

The brand should feel like a premium Christian family office experience, but not family-office-only.

Locked direction:

- Apple-level simplicity
- Mobile first
- Desktop responsive
- Warm white or very light gray/tan background
- Deep navy primary
- Soft gold accent
- Subtle green accent
- Stylized SC monogram placeholder logo
- Avoid shield/cross logo for now
- Avoid overly churchy visuals
- Avoid cluttered fintech dashboard feel

## Audience

The homepage should speak to:

- Christian business owners
- Doctors
- Dentists
- Real estate investors
- Executives
- Retirees
- Affluent Christian families

It should not feel like a budgeting app, financial advisor site, CPA firm, wealth management firm, generic fintech app, or family-office-only brand.

## Homepage Hero

Hero headline:

"Steward What Matters Most."

Subheadline:

"Understand, organize, and grow everything God has entrusted to you from your finances and business to your family, legacy, and impact."

Primary CTA:

Start Your Assessment

Secondary CTA:

View Sample Dashboard

Trust bullets:

- Takes about 5 minutes
- Progress saves automatically

Key copy:

"Clarity brings confidence. Stewardship brings purpose."

Scripture CTA:

"To whom much is given, much is required." Luke 12:48

## Homepage Message

The homepage should make the first step feel safe, simple, and worthwhile.

Users should understand:

- The first assessment is a fast diagnostic.
- It takes about 5 minutes.
- It does not ask for dollar amounts.
- It does not require account connections.
- It does not require uploads.
- Progress saves automatically.
- The dashboard is created from their answers.
- Financial Analysis comes later for deeper opportunities and professional reports.

## Homepage Journey

The homepage should support this journey:

Landing Page -> Create Account -> 5 to 7 minute assessment -> Dashboard Created -> Explore Dashboard -> Complete Financial Analysis -> Unlock Opportunities -> Generate Professional Reports

## Homepage Sections

Recommended first homepage shell:

1. Header with stylized SC monogram placeholder and Stewardship Capital wordmark.
2. Hero with headline, subheadline, CTAs, trust bullets, and scripture CTA.
3. Dashboard preview with score, stage, priorities, and four-pillar snapshot.
4. Four pillars section.
5. Audience section.
6. Financial Analysis and reports preview.
7. Final CTA to Start Your Assessment.

## Dashboard Preview Direction

The dashboard preview should show the product clearly, not just a decorative card.

Preview elements:

- Stewardship Score
- Stewardship Stage
- Profile Completion %
- Protect / Grow / Transfer / Impact snapshot
- Top 3 priorities
- Next recommended step
- CTA to Complete Financial Analysis

## Four Pillars

Use these exact pillar lines:

- Protect what God has entrusted
- Grow your capacity to serve
- Transfer values and resources to future generations
- Impact Kingdom and community

Keep pillar copy elegant, brief, and grounded.

## Visual Guardrails

Use:

- Warm white or very light gray/tan backgrounds
- Deep navy authority color
- Soft gold accents
- Subtle green accents
- Restrained iconography
- Generous whitespace
- Clear typography

Avoid:

- Shields
- Cross logo
- Overly churchy visuals
- Cluttered fintech dashboards
- Generic blue fintech gradients
- Heavy luxury black-and-gold treatment
- Dense financial jargon

## Tone

Voice should be:

- Clear
- Premium
- Christian
- Grounded
- Strategic
- Encouraging

Avoid fear-based financial messaging. Stewardship should feel purposeful, not pressured.
